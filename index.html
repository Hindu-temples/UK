About this data

This directory merges two independent research passes (one from Gemini, one from ChatGPT) and then verifies every entry against Google Places — so temple names, street addresses and map coordinates reflect the actual buildings, not town-centre approximations.

That verification does three things at once: it pins each temple to its real location, it separates temples that the source lists had stacked on a single city coordinate, and it filters out entries that turned out not to be Hindu temples (or, in one case, a temple in Alabama).

A small number of well-known temples that didn't return a clean Places match are included at approximate locations and labelled as such on their card.

Distances are straight-line (as-the-crow-flies) from your postcode, meant for ranking rather than routing — tap Directions on any temple for a real driving route.

Coverage is broad but not exhaustive; national bodies list several hundred places of worship, and community temples open and move. If you spot something missing or out of date, it can be added.
