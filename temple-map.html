Hindu temples by region

London and the Midlands hold the largest clusters, tracing where Britain's Hindu communities first settled — but every UK region now has at least one mandir. The directory spans 86 sanatan / community, 30 saiva / tamil, 14 baps swaminarayan, 12 swaminarayan (other), 9 iskcon / vaishnava.
